About

Hello world! I am Damiano.

I’ve always been fascinated with technology, that’s why I graduated in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome. In my early professional years I have worked as a Web Developer for an occupational safety firm in Rome (IT), where I am originally from.

After moving to London (UK) I worked in managerial roles in the hospitality business. Here I had the chance to apply my coding knowledge to develop applications that would automate various processes and tasks, and integrate with some of the tools that are being used in the industry.

I became passionate about Data Science, led by my curiosity and the desire to understand things, and through rigorous courseworks I have deepened my understanding of Statistics, Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

This website is a place for me to show my personal work, deriving insight from data using a variety of statistical tools and models.
